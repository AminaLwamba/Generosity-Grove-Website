About Us

Generosity Grove is a nonprofit organization dedicated to providing educational opportunities for underprivileged children. Our purpose is to empower these children by covering their school fees, ensuring they have access to quality education and a brighter future. extends its support beyond just financial assistance. We believe in holistic development, which means we also provide mentorship and guidance to the children we support. Through workshops and one-on-one mentorship sessions, we help students develop critical life skills, build confidence, and prepare for a successful future.

Our History

Generosity Grove was established in 2015 by a group of passionate educators, philanthropists, and community leaders. Recognizing the transformative power of education, they aimed to create a platform that would provide sustained financial support to children who otherwise would not be able to attend school. Over the years, Generosity Grove has grown from supporting a handful of students to funding thousands of young minds across the country. Through partnerships with schools, local communities, and international donors, Generosity Grove has become a cornerstone of educational equity, continuously expanding its reach and impact. The heart of Generosity Grove lies in its unwavering commitment to nurturing the potential of every child, ensuring they have the resources and opportunities to achieve their dreams and contribute positively to society.

Our Principles

Mission

We strive for a just world that advances children's rights and equality for girls. Our mission is to empower children, young people, and communities to make vital changes that tackle the root causes of discrimination against girls, exclusion, and vulnerability. We work with passion and dedication to drive change in practice and policy at local, national, and global levels, ensuring that every child has the opportunity to thrive.
