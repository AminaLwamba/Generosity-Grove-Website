About Generosity Grove

Our Mission

Generosity Grove a dedicated charity that provides educational opportunities to underprivileged children by covering school fees, supplying educational materials, and offering mentorship programs. Through funding coordination, capacity building, and advocacy, we aim to raise awareness, collect donations, and recruit volunteers to support our mission.
